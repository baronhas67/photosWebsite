About the Page

This website is about a history of personal "Minecraft" gameplay, with my cousin in a free multiplayer world. Some of the favourite Minecraft screenshots are displayed on the page with some very short description. We love to capture the memories so that it can be seen again after a long time.

Why?

I am not a professional or even an intermediate web developer. But when I and my cousin were playing on a fresh world and we got really interested and made some great progress. So one day I decided to practice my skills and created this website. I still upload the images manually and they are compressed to save space.
